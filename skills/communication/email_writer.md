# Email Writer Skill

## Description
Professional email composition assistant for business and personal communication.

## Capabilities

### Writing Styles
- Formal business correspondence
- Casual team updates
- Cold outreach emails
- Follow-up messages
- Thank you notes
- Apology letters

### Features
- Tone adjustment
- Grammar optimization
- Subject line suggestions
- Call-to-action optimization
- Personalization

## Configuration

```yaml
skill_id: email_writer_v1
version: 1.0.0
templates:
  - formal
  - casual
  - persuasive
  - apologetic
integrations:
  - gmail
  - outlook
  - smtp
```

- Base rate: 0.0005 BEE per email
